# Case Study 1 – Netflix

**Organization:** Netflix

**Business Problem:**
Netflix needed to understand viewer preferences to recommend relevant content and improve user retention.

**BI Solution:**
Netflix analyzed user viewing history, watch time, ratings, and search behavior using Business Intelligence and data analytics. Interactive dashboards helped monitor content performance and customer engagement.

**Business Impact:**
Personalized recommendations increased user engagement, improved customer retention, and supported data-driven decisions for content production and licensing.

**Key Learning:**
Business Intelligence can transform customer behavior data into actionable insights that improve user experience and business growth.

---

# Case Study 2 – Starbucks

**Organization:** Starbucks

**Business Problem:**
Choosing profitable locations for new stores while understanding customer purchasing patterns.

**BI Solution:**
Starbucks used Business Intelligence by combining demographic data, customer transactions, market research, and geographic information to identify ideal store locations and optimize inventory.

**Business Impact:**
Improved site selection, increased sales, reduced operational risks, and enhanced customer satisfaction.

**Key Learning:**
Combining multiple data sources enables organizations to make informed strategic decisions rather than relying on intuition.

---

# Case Study 3 – Airbnb

**Organization:** Airbnb

**Business Problem:**
Airbnb needed to monitor guest experiences, host performance, and booking trends across millions of listings.

**BI Solution:**
Airbnb utilized Business Intelligence dashboards to analyze reviews, occupancy rates, pricing trends, booking behavior, and customer satisfaction metrics.

**Business Impact:**
Hosts received actionable insights to improve their listings, while Airbnb optimized pricing strategies and enhanced the overall user experience.

**Key Learning:**
Analyzing customer feedback alongside operational metrics enables continuous service improvement and better business performance.
